Herbal teas for everyday calm

Mar 5, 2025 • Tags: calming, teas

Herbal teas can be a gentle way to support relaxation. Chamomile (Matricaria recutita), lemon balm (Melissa officinalis), and passionflower (Passiflora incarnata) are commonly used — but check drug-herb interactions and individual sensitivities before regular use.
